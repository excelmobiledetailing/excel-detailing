Our Story

Started From a Driveway.

Excel Mobile Detailing started the way most great things do — with a car that needed cleaning and a couple of guys who refused to do a half-hearted job. What began as detailing cars for family and friends quickly turned into something bigger when word spread about the quality of our work.

We're students at Glenbrook North High School, and while other people our age are watching TV, we're out in driveways learning our craft, one car at a time. We take pride in treating every vehicle like it's our own — whether it's a daily driver or a Porsche Turbo S.

We started this business because we love cars. Simple as that. And when you love what you do, it shows in the results.

The Process

How We Learned Our Craft

We didn't just pick up a sponge and call it detailing. We studied the techniques, invested in the right products, and put in real hours to get it right.

01
Countless Hours of Research

We spent months watching professional detailers, studying paint correction techniques, safe wash methods, and interior restoration — learning what actually works versus what just looks good on the surface.

02
Hands-On Practice

Theory only gets you so far. We detailed car after car — starting with our own vehicles and family members' cars — refining our technique with every job until the results spoke for themselves.

03
Professional Products Only

We use the same professional-grade soaps, microfiber towels, interior cleaners, and protectants used by high-end detail shops — because the products you use matter just as much as the technique.
